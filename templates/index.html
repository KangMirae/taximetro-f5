About Digital Taximeter Simulator

This application simulates a real taxi meter system with time-based fare calculation. The fare is dynamically updated depending on the taxi state (moving or stopped), selected fare level, and optional surcharges such as out-of-city or night rates. All calculations are handled on the server to ensure accuracy and consistency, while the interface reflects real-time updates. Fare rules and applied options are intentionally revealed only at the end of the trip, mimicking real-world taxi behavior. Trip history is automatically stored and can be reviewed at any time.
